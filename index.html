Natural fibres * Botanical Dyes * Sustainable Crafts

The cross between the fascinating world of natural dyeing with a circular economy approach to sourcing raw materials. Our goal is to create sustainable and colorful crafts supplies that allow artisans and artists to be eco-friendlier in their creations.

I am a biologist by training and I always had an interest in environmental issues and solutions. My love of crafting led me to source more biodegradable materials, less harmful dyes and rely on what nature provides.
